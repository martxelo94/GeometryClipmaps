-Run project with VS2017 x86 Windows10

-Executable outputed to bin/ folder.

-Shaders at data/shaders/ folder.

-F9 to unlock frame-rate.

-Press Ass* button at top-left to RESET scene.

- Camera controls:
	RMB: rotate FP camera.
	Wheel: Move forward/backward.
	W:     Move forward XZ plane.
	S:     Move backward XZ plane.
	A,D:   Side movement xZ plane.
	Q:     Move down Y.
	E:     Move up Y.


-Use GUI to change several properties or load other scenes.
